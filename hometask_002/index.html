  • Los Santos Vagos

    Originally LSV were two Hispanic American gangs (East Side Vagos and Northside Mexicans) that existed independently. Nowadays, they go by a Spanish translation of "the lazy saints".

    As there are no so-called "sets", the information will be presented as an article.

    Territory: Los Flores, Las Colinas, East Los Santos, East Beach.

    Occupation: Street drug dealing, drug trafficking, fighting against Varrios Los Aztecas.

    Known gang members:

    Freddy(?) - ex-gangster who was released from the state penitentiary.

    Big Poppa(?) - major drug dealer. Blackmails Vinewood celebrities hoping to have a slice.

  • Varrios Los Aztecas

    Mexican refugees who did not support LSV in their willing to take over the Los Santos illegal market. Basing in southern city districts, they don't interfere other gangs' operations.

    Same as LSV, VLA do not have set division.

    Territory: El Corona, Little Mexico.

    Occupation: Lowrider festivals, street racing.

    Known gang members:

    Cesar Vialpando - one of the most estimated and dangerous members of the family.

    Jose(?) - one of the "veteranos" - experienced soldiers in turf war.

    Sunny(?), Hazer(?) - two brothers, also weapon afficionados and loyal executives.
